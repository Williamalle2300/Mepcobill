About MEPCO

MEPCO, short for Multan Electric Power Company, is the largest electricity distribution company in Pakistan's South Punjab region. Established in 1998, it serves over 34 million customers across 13 districts. These districts include Multan, Muzaffargarh, Bahawalpur, and others.

MEPCO's primary goal is to ensure uninterrupted electricity supply to homes, businesses, and industries. In addition to providing electricity, MEPCO also offers a streamlined billing system that makes it easy for consumers to stay on top of their monthly usage and payments.

Steps to Check Your MEPCO Bill Online

Follow these simple steps to view your MEPCO bill online:

  1. Enter your 14-digit reference number or your 10-digit consumer ID in the designated field.
  2. Click on the "Submit" button to retrieve your bill.
  3. Once the bill appears, you have the option to download or print it directly from the webpage.

Understanding the MEPCO Bill

The MEPCO bill is divided into several sections, which include:

  • Consumer Information: This section provides details about the consumer, such as their name, address, and reference number.
  • Billing Period: The dates for which the bill is being issued, usually covering one month.
  • Current Charges: Shows how much electricity has been consumed during the billing period and the associated cost.
  • Due Date: The last date by which payment can be made without incurring any penalties.
  • Total Charges: A summary of all the charges, including taxes, fees, and any additional costs.

How to Avoid Late Payments

Missing the due date on your electricity bill can lead to fines and even disconnection. To avoid late payments:

  • Set reminders on your phone or calendar a few days before the due date.
  • Consider setting up automatic payments through your bank to ensure timely payments.
  • Regularly check your bill online so that you're aware of any updates or changes in your usage or charges.
